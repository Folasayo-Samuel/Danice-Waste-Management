About Us

Danice was founded in 2005.We are licensed by the City of Tshwane to Collect Waste and Recycling , we are also Members of Institute of Waste Management of South Africa ( IWMSA) Our passion for the clean and green environments puts us in the fore front of the waste management practices and recycling business in SA. We provide unparalleled waste collection and recycling services than any other competitor out there. We have developed a system over the years on how we can effectively serve our clients’ demand with modern tools and a high level of professionalism. At the core of our business is a deep passion for cleanliness and a love for green economy. Danice Waste Management and Recycling has consistent driven the need for Job creation through our recycling programs, we are the only Waste Collection Company in Tshwane that offers both Waste Collection and Recycling programs in the inner city. Our Recycling Program has enabled us to create more employment as compared to our competitors. because we offer Recycling with Waste Collection, we need 36 workers to complete the Waste Collection and Disposal cycle, as compared to our competitors who only require 6 workers to complete only the waste collection cycle. Our core values of Tolerance and understanding among all staff and our business partners. Our mission is to be reduce the unemployment rate in our community by engaging them in our recycling programs.
